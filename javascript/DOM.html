Spider-Man

About

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962).

Creation & Development

In 1962, with the success of the Fantastic Four, Marvel editor Stan Lee was looking for a new superhero idea. The idea for Spider-Man arose from teenage demand for comic books and the desire to create a character teenagers could identify with.
