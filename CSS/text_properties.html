Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the best cricketers in the world. He is known for his aggressive batting, consistency, and strong mindset on the field.

Early Life

He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. From a young age, he was passionate about cricket and worked hard to build his career.

Career

Kohli made his debut for the Indian team in 2008. Over the years, he became one of the top players and also captained the team in all formats.
